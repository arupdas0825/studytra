# Scholarships in Austria for International Students (2026)

## Overview
While tuition fees at public universities are very low (€726.72/semester), international students can apply for grants and scholarships to cover their monthly living costs. Most grants are administered by the OeAD (Austrian Agency for Education and Internationalisation).

---

## Key Scholarships & Grants (2026)

### 1. Ernst Mach Grant (UAS / Fachhochschule)
- **Funding:** €1,050 per month + waiver of tuition fees.
- **Target Audience:** Non-EU/EEA students enrolled in a Master's program at an Austrian University of Applied Sciences (Fachhochschule).
- **Eligibility:** Aged under 35, completed a Bachelor's degree, and must have received an admission offer or be nominated by the UAS.
- **Application Deadline:** March 1, 2026 (for Winter intake 2026/2027).
- **Official Portal:** https://grants.at

### 2. Technology Scholarships Klagenfurt (AAU)
- **Funding:** €700 per month + full waiver of tuition fees. Travel allowance up to €400 is also provided.
- **Target Audience:** Master's students in Technical Sciences (Informatics, AI, Information Technology, Systems Design, Mathematics).
- **Eligibility:** English IELTS 6.5 minimum, Bachelor in a related engineering field, non-EU status.
- **Application Deadline:** Typically mid-March 2026.
- **Official Portal:** https://www.aau.at

### 3. Richard Plastka Scholarship
- **Funding:** Up to €600 per month for study support.
- **Target Audience:** Degree-seeking students at public Austrian universities based on performance and economic need.
- **Deadline:** Varies by university.

### 4. OeAD Franz Werfel Grant
- **Funding:** €1,050 per month + library access and health insurance assistance.
- **Target Audience:** Material research, young university teachers of German language and Austrian literature.
- **Deadline:** March 1 and September 15 annually.
