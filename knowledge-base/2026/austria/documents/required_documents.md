# Required Documents for Austria Study Application & Visa (2026)

## Overview
Austrian authorities require all documents from non-EU countries (such as India) to be legalized (Apostilled) and officially translated into German or English.

---

## 1. University Admission Phase

Students must submit the following to the university admission department (TISS, u-space, TUGRAZonline, etc.):
- **Valid Passport:** Copy of the data page.
- **Academic Transcripts:** Complete mark sheets of secondary education (10th/12th) or university Bachelor transcripts.
- **Degree Certificate / Diploma:** Provisional or final degree certificates.
- **Apostille:** All Indian educational certificates must be apostilled by the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA), Government of India, before submission.
- **Proof of Special University Entrance Qualification (Universitätsreife):** A mandatory requirement for public universities. This is a certificate proving that you are eligible to study the exact same degree program in India. Acceptable proofs:
  - An admission letter for the same program from a recognized Indian university, OR
  - A certificate from a recognized Indian university stating you would be admitted to this course, OR
  - Passing JEE Mains/Advanced (for technical programs at TU Wien/TU Graz).
- **Language Proficiency Proof:**
  - For English programs: IELTS Academic (6.5), TOEFL iBT (87), or PTE (62).
  - For German programs: Goethe or ÖSD certificate at C1 level.
- **Syllabus / Course Description:** A detailed booklet describing the content, books, and hours of each subject studied in your Bachelor's degree (required for credit equivalence check in Masters).

---

## 2. Residence Permit Phase (Visa)

Once you receive your Admission Letter (Zulassungsbescheid), you must submit the following to the Austrian Embassy in New Delhi:
- **Application Form:** Completed and signed "Antragsformular Aufenthaltsbewilligung Student".
- **Valid Passport:** Must be valid for the entire duration of the permit or at least 15 months.
- **ICAO Passport Photos:** Two recent biometric color photos.
- **Birth Certificate:** Legalized with an MEA Apostille and translated into German.
- **Police Clearance Certificate (PCC):** Issued by the Regional Passport Office (RPO) in India. Must be legalized with an MEA Apostille, translated, and be less than 3 months old at submission.
- **Admission Letter (Zulassungsbescheid):** The official university admission letter.
- **Proof of Accommodation:** A legally binding rental agreement (e.g. OeAD housing contract, STUWO rental contract, or WG lease).
- **Proof of Financial Means:** Bank statements of the last 6 months + proof of source of funds (salary slips, IT returns of sponsor).
- **Travel Health Insurance:** Covering at least €30,000 and valid for the first 1-2 months in Austria until you enroll and get the ÖGK student insurance.
