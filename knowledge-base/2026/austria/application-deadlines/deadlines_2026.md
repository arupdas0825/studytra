# Application Deadlines for Austrian Universities (2026)

## Overview
Austrian universities have separate deadlines for EU and non-EU citizens. Non-EU/EEA citizens (such as Indian applicants) must apply significantly earlier to allow 3-4 months for the visa processing.

## Crucial Timeline Advice
1. **Documents Verification:** Start collecting transcripts and applying for MEA Apostilles in India at least 3 months prior to deadlines (April for Winter intake, October for Summer intake).
2. **Postage Transit:** For public universities requiring physical mail submission (like TU Wien or TU Graz), ensure your document package is dispatched via DHL/FedEx at least 15 days before the July 15 / January 15 deadlines.
