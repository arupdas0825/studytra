# Medical University of Vienna

Medizinische Universität Wien (MedUni Wien)

## City
Vienna

## State
Vienna

## Public / Private
Public

## QS Ranking
Top 100 globally in Medicine

## THE Ranking
201-250 (THE World University Rankings 2026)

[Medical University of Vienna](https://www.meduniwien.ac.at/web/en/)

[International Office](https://www.meduniwien.ac.at/web/en/studies/studying-internationally/)

[Admission Portal](https://www.meduniwien.ac.at/web/en/studies/admissions/)

## Official Email
studienabteilung@meduniwien.ac.at

## Overview
The largest medical organization in Austria, linked to the Vienna General Hospital.

## History
Established in 1365 as the medical faculty of Univ. of Vienna. Independent in 2004.

## Campus
AKH hospital campus, Vienna.

## Popular Faculties
Human Medicine, Dentistry, Medical Biotechnology.

## Popular Courses
Dr. med. univ. (Medicine), Dr. med. dent. (Dentistry).

## English-taught Programs
PhD programs and clinical research training are in English.

## German-taught Programs
Doctor of Medicine degrees require German C1 and passing MedAT exam.

## Degree Levels
Bachelor (BSc/BA), Master (MSc/MA), PhD (Doctoral).

## Application Intakes
Winter Semester (Starts October) and Summer Semester (Starts March).

## Application Deadlines (2026)
National MedAT registration closes in March. Exam in July.

## Eligibility
- **Bachelor (Undergraduate):** Secondary school certificate (CBSE, ICSE, or State boards) + Special University Entrance Qualification (proof of eligibility for the same course in India).
- **Master (Postgraduate):** A relevant Bachelor's degree from a recognized Indian university, with core credits matching the curriculum.

## Minimum GPA
Minimum GPA equivalent to 2.5 on the Austrian scale (~60% minimum average).

## IELTS
IELTS Academic 6.5 overall (minimum 6.0 in all bands) for English-taught courses.

## TOEFL
TOEFL iBT 87 minimum.

## PTE
PTE Academic 62 minimum.

## Duolingo
Official information not publicly available. Standard tests (IELTS/TOEFL) are required.

## GRE
Not required, but recommended for technical programs to strengthen the application profile.

## GMAT
Not required (except for select quantitative business master programs).

## Application Fee
Free (Standard university application portal is free of charge).

## Tuition Fee
€726.72 per semester for non-EU/EEA students.

## Semester Fee
€24.70 per semester ÖH Student Union fee (2026). Total semester cost: €751.42 (Public) or €1,524.70 (UAS).

## Scholarships
Merit and performance-based scholarships are available from the university. Ernst Mach Grants are available for UAS students via OeAD (grants.at).

## Accommodation
OeAD Student Housing, STUWO, Milestone, or private shared apartments (WGs). Monthly rent: €300 – €550.

## Living Cost
Estimated at €850 – €1,200 per month depending on the city (Graz/Leoben are cheaper, Vienna/Innsbruck are more expensive).

## Student Jobs
Part-time student jobs up to 20 hours/week. German language skills are highly recommended for non-tech jobs.

## Internship Opportunities
Mandatory industrial internships are integrated into most mechatronics and computing curricula. Styria, Linz, and Vienna offer abundant tech roles.

## Research Opportunities
Strong research programs in cyber-physical systems, mechatronics, quantum physics, molecular biology, and applied geoinformatics.

## Required Documents
1. Passport Copy
2. Academic Transcripts (Apostilled by Ministry of External Affairs, India)
3. Degree Certificates (Apostilled)
4. Syllabus / Module descriptions of previous studies
5. Language Proficiency Proof (IELTS or German Goethe/ÖSD C1)
6. Proof of Special University Entrance Qualification (for non-EU)
7. Translated documents (German or English).

## Application Process
1. Apply online via the respective admission portal.
2. Send certified physical copies of legalized documents by post if requested.
3. The university performs equivalence checks (6-12 weeks).
4. Zulassungsbescheid (Admission Letter) is sent by post.
5. Apply for the Student Residence Permit at the Embassy.

## Visa Support
Welcome Center and International Office provide manuals and general support.

## Health Insurance
ÖGK Student Self-insurance (€69.13 per month in 2026).

## Graduate Employability
Extremely high. Graduates qualify for the Red-White-Red Card for Graduates to transition into full-time employment.
