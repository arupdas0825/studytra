# Economics in Austria (2026)

## Overview
Focuses on microeconomics, macroeconomics, econometrics, public policy, and quantitative methods.

## Top Universities Offering Economics
University of Vienna, WU Vienna, University of Graz, JKU Linz.

## Duration & Degree Structure
Master: 2 years.

## Language of Instruction
English-medium.

## Key Eligibility & Language Requirements
Bachelor in Economics/Mathematics/Statistics. IELTS 6.5/7.0. Quantitative GRE is recommended.

## Career & Employment Prospects (Red-White-Red Card)
Strong. Financial consulting, research institutions (WIFO, IHS), central banks, and public administration.
