# Medicine in Austria (2026)

## Overview
Medical degrees in Austria (Human Medicine) are highly prestigious and require a 6-year integrated curriculum.

## Top Universities Offering Medicine
Medical University of Vienna, Medical University of Innsbruck, Medical University of Graz, JKU Linz.

## Duration & Degree Structure
6 years (Dr. med. univ.).

## Language of Instruction
German-only.

## Key Eligibility & Language Requirements
Apostilled high school diploma + passing the MedAT entrance exam in July. C1 German is required.

## Career & Employment Prospects (Red-White-Red Card)
100% employment. Graduates directly practice in Austrian and European hospitals.
