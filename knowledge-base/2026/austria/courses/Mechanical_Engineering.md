# Mechanical Engineering in Austria (2026)

## Overview
Mechanical Engineering in Austria focuses on automotive design, manufacturing systems, fluid mechanics, and thermodynamics.

## Top Universities Offering Mechanical Engineering
TU Wien, TU Graz, University of Leoben, FH Joanneum, FH Upper Austria (Wels).

## Duration & Degree Structure
Bachelor: 3 years | Master: 2 years.

## Language of Instruction
Bachelors are in German. Several Masters are in English (especially automotive/materials focused).

## Key Eligibility & Language Requirements
Bachelor in Mechanical Engineering. IELTS 6.5 (for English progs) or Goethe C1 (for German).

## Career & Employment Prospects (Red-White-Red Card)
Very High. Austria's strong automotive and industrial base (AVL, Magna, KTM) recruits directly from these universities.
