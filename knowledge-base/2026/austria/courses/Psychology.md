# Psychology in Austria (2026)

## Overview
Covers clinical psychology, cognitive neuroscience, social psychology, and business psychology.

## Top Universities Offering Psychology
University of Vienna, University of Graz, University of Innsbruck, University of Salzburg.

## Duration & Degree Structure
Bachelor: 3 years | Master: 2 years.

## Language of Instruction
Bachelors are in German (requires entrance exam). Masters require German C1.

## Key Eligibility & Language Requirements
Entrance exam + German C1.

## Career & Employment Prospects (Red-White-Red Card)
Strong. Clinical practice (requires postgraduate training), corporate HR, consulting, and research.
