# Cyber Security in Austria (2026)

## Overview
Cyber Security programs focus on securing networks, cryptography, mobile security, ethical hacking, and secure software development.

## Top Universities Offering Cyber Security
FH Joanneum, FH Upper Austria (Hagenberg), FH Technikum Wien, TU Graz, TU Wien.

## Duration & Degree Structure
Master: 2 years (120 ECTS).

## Language of Instruction
Mainly English at Master level, some FH programs are in German.

## Key Eligibility & Language Requirements
Bachelor in CS or network engineering. IELTS 6.5.

## Career & Employment Prospects (Red-White-Red Card)
Very high. Security Analysts, Network Auditors, and Cryptographers are in extreme demand across public and private sectors in Austria.
