# Law in Austria (2026)

## Overview
Focuses on Austrian civil law, penal law, public law, and European Union jurisprudence.

## Top Universities Offering Law
University of Vienna, University of Graz, University of Innsbruck, WU Vienna (Business Law), JKU Linz.

## Duration & Degree Structure
Integrated 4-year diploma (Mag. iur.) or Bachelor+Master (WU Business Law).

## Language of Instruction
German-only.

## Key Eligibility & Language Requirements
Apostilled school leaving certificate. German C1 certificate.

## Career & Employment Prospects (Red-White-Red Card)
Strong. Corporate legal advisors, public administration, or bar qualification (requires Austrian citizenship for judicial clerkships).
