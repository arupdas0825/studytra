# Dentistry in Austria (2026)

## Overview
Studies focus on dental surgery, orthodontics, and oral pathology over a 6-year integrated course.

## Top Universities Offering Dentistry
Medical University of Vienna, Medical University of Innsbruck, Medical University of Graz.

## Duration & Degree Structure
6 years (Dr. med. dent.).

## Language of Instruction
German-only.

## Key Eligibility & Language Requirements
High school leaving diploma + passing the MedAT-Z exam. C1 German.

## Career & Employment Prospects (Red-White-Red Card)
100%. Licensed to open private clinics or practice in dental clinics in the EU.
