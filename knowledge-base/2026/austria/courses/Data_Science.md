# Data_Science in Austria (2026)

## Overview
Data Science programs combine advanced computer science, statistics, and domain-specific analytics to turn large datasets into actionable insights.

## Top Universities Offering Data_Science
University of Vienna, TU Wien, TU Graz, JKU Linz, FH Salzburg, FH Upper Austria.

## Duration & Degree Structure
Master: 2 years (120 ECTS).

## Language of Instruction
Master programs are taught in English.

## Key Eligibility & Language Requirements
Bachelor in CS, statistics, math, or quantitative business (WU). Quantitative GRE or background proof is beneficial. IELTS 6.5 / TOEFL 90.

## Career & Employment Prospects (Red-White-Red Card)
Excellent. High demand in banking, telecom, tech, and pharmaceutical companies in Vienna and Linz.
