# MBA in Austria (2026)

## Overview
MBA programs in Austria focus on international leadership, entrepreneurship, finance, and management in European contexts.

## Top Universities Offering MBA
WU Vienna (WU Executive Academy), University of Vienna, MCI Innsbruck, FH Kufstein.

## Duration & Degree Structure
1 to 2 years.

## Language of Instruction
English.

## Key Eligibility & Language Requirements
Bachelor's degree, 3-5 years of post-graduation professional work experience, GMAT/GRE (required for elite programs), IELTS 7.0.

## Career & Employment Prospects (Red-White-Red Card)
Outstanding. Highly sought in consultancy, multinational management, and corporate strategy roles in Vienna.
