# Biotechnology in Austria (2026)

## Overview
Biotechnology studies cover molecular biology, fermentation tech, bioinformatics, and food security.

## Top Universities Offering Biotechnology
BOKU University, University of Vienna, FH Campus Wien, MCI Innsbruck, FH Upper Austria.

## Duration & Degree Structure
Bachelor: 3 years | Master: 2 years.

## Language of Instruction
Masters are widely available in English.

## Key Eligibility & Language Requirements
Bachelor in biotech, chemistry, biochemistry, or biology. IELTS 6.5.

## Career & Employment Prospects (Red-White-Red Card)
Strong. Grad placements in pharmaceutical companies (Boehringer Ingelheim, Takeda) in Vienna and Linz.
