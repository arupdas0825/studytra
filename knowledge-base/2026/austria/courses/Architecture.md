# Architecture in Austria (2026)

## Overview
Austria is famous for sustainable and alpine architecture. Studies focus on design, urban planning, and building technology.

## Top Universities Offering Architecture
TU Wien, TU Graz, University of Innsbruck, University of Applied Arts Vienna.

## Duration & Degree Structure
Bachelor: 3 years | Master: 2 years.

## Language of Instruction
Mainly German-medium (requires C1). Portfolio review is mandatory for admission.

## Key Eligibility & Language Requirements
Bachelor in Architecture, Portfolio of work, C1 German certificate.

## Career & Employment Prospects (Red-White-Red Card)
Strong. Architecture studios and urban planning agencies in major cities.
