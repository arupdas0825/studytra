# Computer Science in Austria (2026)

## Overview
Computer Science is a high-growth field in Austria. Austrian public universities and FHs offer state-of-the-art curriculum, heavily focused on practical labs, software architectures, algorithms, and distributed networks.

## Top Universities Offering Computer Science
TU Wien, TU Graz, University of Vienna, JKU Linz, University of Innsbruck, University of Klagenfurt, FH Technikum Wien, FH Campus Wien, FH Upper Austria.

## Duration & Degree Structure
Bachelor: 3 years (180 ECTS) | Master: 2 years (120 ECTS) | PhD: 3-4 years.

## Language of Instruction
Bachelor programs are mostly taught in German. Master programs (MSc) are widely available in English.

## Key Eligibility & Language Requirements
Master: Bachelor in CS or closely related mechatronics/math fields with sufficient ECTS in programming, theory, and math. English IELTS 6.5 (or TOEFL 87 / PTE 62).

## Career & Employment Prospects (Red-White-Red Card)
High demand. Graduates are recruited as Software Engineers, Systems Architects, and IT Consultants. Eligible for the Red-White-Red Card upon landing a qualifying offer.
