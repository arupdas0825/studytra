# Electrical Engineering in Austria (2026)

## Overview
Electrical Engineering covers power electronics, smart grids, telecommunications, microelectronics, and control systems.

## Top Universities Offering Electrical Engineering
TU Wien, TU Graz, FH Upper Austria (Wels), FH Technikum Wien.

## Duration & Degree Structure
Bachelor: 3 years | Master: 2 years.

## Language of Instruction
Bachelors are in German. Several Masters are in English.

## Key Eligibility & Language Requirements
Bachelor in Electrical Engineering / Electronics. IELTS 6.5 or equivalent.

## Career & Employment Prospects (Red-White-Red Card)
Very high. Austria's energy transition and semiconductor firms (Infineon, NXP) in Villach/Graz actively recruit grads.
