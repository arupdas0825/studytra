# Civil Engineering in Austria (2026)

## Overview
Studies focus on structural engineering, geotechnics, transport systems, and sustainable construction in alpine environments.

## Top Universities Offering Civil Engineering
TU Wien, TU Graz, University of Innsbruck, FH Campus Wien, FH Kärnten.

## Duration & Degree Structure
Bachelor: 3 years | Master: 2 years.

## Language of Instruction
Mainly German-medium. C1 German proficiency is standard.

## Key Eligibility & Language Requirements
Bachelor in Civil Engineering. Special university entrance qualification from India. C1 German certificate.

## Career & Employment Prospects (Red-White-Red Card)
Strong. Alpine construction, railway design (ÖBB), and structural firms recruit grads directly.
