# Biomedical Engineering in Austria (2026)

## Overview
Biomedical Engineering integrates engineering principles with medicine: medical devices, prosthetics, and medical imaging.

## Top Universities Offering Biomedical Engineering
TU Wien, TU Graz, FH Upper Austria (Linz), FH Technikum Wien, MCI Innsbruck.

## Duration & Degree Structure
Master: 2 years (120 ECTS).

## Language of Instruction
Mainly English-taught at Master level.

## Key Eligibility & Language Requirements
Bachelor in engineering or physics. IELTS 6.5.

## Career & Employment Prospects (Red-White-Red Card)
High. Medical device manufacturers and research hospitals (AKH Wien, LKH Graz) recruit grads.
