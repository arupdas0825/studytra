# Finance in Austria (2026)

## Overview
Advanced studies in corporate finance, financial markets, risk management, and quantitative asset pricing.

## Top Universities Offering Finance
WU Vienna, University of Vienna, University of Graz.

## Duration & Degree Structure
Master: 2 years (120 ECTS).

## Language of Instruction
Fully English-taught Master at WU Vienna.

## Key Eligibility & Language Requirements
Bachelor in business/economics/math with quantitative core. GMAT focus 565+ or equivalent GRE is highly recommended for WU. IELTS 7.0.

## Career & Employment Prospects (Red-White-Red Card)
Outstanding. Placements in European banking sector, risk firms, and multinational corporate finance.
