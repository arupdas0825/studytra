# Software Engineering in Austria (2026)

## Overview
Software Engineering focuses on the lifecycle of software systems: design, code quality, agile management, testing, and cloud architectures.

## Top Universities Offering Software Engineering
TU Wien, TU Graz, JKU Linz, FH Technikum Wien, FH Joanneum.

## Duration & Degree Structure
Master: 2 years (120 ECTS).

## Language of Instruction
Masters are mostly in English; Bachelors are in German.

## Key Eligibility & Language Requirements
Bachelor in CS or Software Engineering. IELTS 6.5.

## Career & Employment Prospects (Red-White-Red Card)
Outstanding. Immediate hire post-graduation across Austria, Switzerland, and southern Germany.
