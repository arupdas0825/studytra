# Artificial Intelligence in Austria (2026)

## Overview
Austria is a central European research hub for AI and machine learning, particularly at JKU Linz which pioneered key neural network layers (LSTM). Studies focus on deep learning, reinforcement learning, NLP, and computer vision.

## Top Universities Offering Artificial Intelligence
JKU Linz, University of Klagenfurt, TU Wien, TU Graz.

## Duration & Degree Structure
Master: 2 years (120 ECTS) | PhD: 3-4 years.

## Language of Instruction
Master programs are taught fully in English.

## Key Eligibility & Language Requirements
Bachelor in CS, mathematics, or physics. Solid foundation in linear algebra, statistics, and programming. IELTS 6.5 or equivalent.

## Career & Employment Prospects (Red-White-Red Card)
Excellent. Highly recruited as ML Engineers, AI Researchers, and Data Analysts in tech hubs like Linz, Vienna, and Graz.
