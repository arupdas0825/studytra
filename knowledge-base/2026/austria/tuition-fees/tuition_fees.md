# Tuition Fees in Austria (2026)

## Overview
Tuition fees in Austria are highly regulated. For public universities, the fee is set by federal law. For Universities of Applied Sciences (Fachhochschulen / FHs), fees vary by university and nationality. Private universities set their own rates.

## Health Insurance Fees (2026)
- **Student Self-Insurance (ÖGK):** €69.13 per month. This is mandatory for all international students holding a Student Residence Permit and must be paid directly to the Österreichische Gesundheitskasse (ÖGK).

## Additional Fees
- **ÖH-Beitrag (Student Union Fee):** €24.70 per semester (mandatory for all enrolled students).
- **Document Translations & Legalization (India):** €150 – €300 (Apostille and certified German/English translation costs in India).
- **Visa / Residence Permit Fees:** €160 (Residence Permit Application fee: €120 upon submission, €20 upon granting, €20 card fee).
